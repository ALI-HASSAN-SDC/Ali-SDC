// FAQ

Common Questions

What does the free consultation include?
▾
A 30-minute call where I review your current Aconex setup or document control workflow, identify the top 3 bottlenecks, and give you a clear picture of what's fixable and how long it would take. No pitch, no pressure — just an honest assessment.
Do I need to give you access to my Aconex system?
▾
For configuration and workflow work, yes — admin-level access scoped to your project. For tool deployments and dashboards, I can work entirely from your data exports without live system access. We'll agree on the right scope during the initial call.
How long does a typical Aconex setup take?
▾
A full configuration — numbering schema, workflows, access control, templates — typically takes 1–2 weeks depending on project complexity and stakeholder count. Focused scopes (workflow optimization, metadata tool deployment) can be completed in 3–5 days.
Can your tools work with any Aconex project?
▾
Yes. All tools are fully configurable — numbering schemas, field mappings, and naming conventions are project-specific inputs, not hard-coded. I configure the tool for your exact convention during deployment, and you'll be able to adjust it independently afterward.
Do you offer ongoing support after delivery?
▾
Yes. Most projects include a 30-day support window post-delivery. For teams managing live systems or using automation tools in production, I offer retainer arrangements for ongoing support, updates, and training.
Are you available for short-term or phase-specific engagements?
▾
Absolutely. I take both fixed-scope deliverable projects and time-based engagements for a specific project phase. Tell me your situation and we'll find the right structure — whether that's a 1-week tool deployment or a 3-month embedded DC role.
